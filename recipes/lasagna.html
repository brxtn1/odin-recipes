Cereal Recipe


Picture of a Bowl of Cereal

Best Cereal in the DMV, extremely well known recipe around the world, uses Frosted Cheerios, 2% Milk, a bowl and a spoon.



Ingredients


  • Frosted Cheerios
  • 2% White Milk
  • A Bowl
  • A Spoon

  1. Get your Bowl
  2. Pour the Cheerios in the Bowl
  3. Pour the 2% Milk into the Bowl
  4. Get your Spoon and Eat
